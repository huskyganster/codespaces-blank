SHRUBB BREEDING

On Plant Island, the double-element Monster Shrubb is initially accessible. The best way to get it is to cross Potbelly and Noggin. Its breeding cycle lasts 8 hours by default, or 6 hours if you have the Enhanced Breeding Structure. Shrubb has a low currency production for a Double Elemental.

BREED CHANCE

100%

  • + Potbelly Noggin
